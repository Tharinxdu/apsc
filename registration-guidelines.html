Registration Guidelines

Registration details, fees, and important information for APSC 2026

Registration Information

Please complete the registration form to secure your participation in the Asia Pacific Stroke Conference 2026. Registration fees vary according to your country income group and participant category.

To check your country’s income classification: Click here

Registration Fees

The following fee structure is subject to final confirmation by the organizing committee.

Category Low-Income Countries Middle-Income Countries High-Income Countries
Consultants / Specialists USD 120 USD 180 USD 250
Medical Officers USD 80 USD 120 USD 160
Nurses / Allied Health Professionals USD 50 USD 80 USD 120
Students / Trainees USD 30 USD 50 USD 80

Payment Instructions

All registration fees must be paid in USD only. Upon completing the registration form, you will be directed to the payment portal.

Your registration will be confirmed only after the payment is successfully processed.

Cancellation Policy

All cancellations must be communicated via email to the Conference Secretariat.

  • Refunds are available for cancellation requests received on or before 31 August 2026.
  • A cancellation fee of 30% will be deducted to cover administrative costs.
  • No refunds will be issued for cancellations received after 31 August 2026.
  • No refunds for no-shows.

All refund procedures will be handled through the official online registration system after the conference.
